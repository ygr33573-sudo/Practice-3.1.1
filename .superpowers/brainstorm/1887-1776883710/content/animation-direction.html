What animation feel fits Cabinet Dr. Benali?

The site is a professional French medical clinic. Animations should support trust and clarity — not distract. Pick the direction that feels right.

A

Subtle & Institutional

Gentle fade-ins and soft upward slides when sections enter the viewport. No bouncing, no spring physics. Everything feels calm, deliberate, and trustworthy — like a high-end clinic brochure come to life.

  • Fade + slight Y-translate on scroll-enter
  • Staggered card reveals (0.08s between each)
  • Smooth hover lifts on cards (already there)
  • No number counters, no scale pops
B

Modern & Engaging

Scroll-triggered reveals with a bit more energy — number counters on stats, a subtle parallax drift on the hero image, and a gentle floating pulse on the hero badge. Still professional, but with visible motion DNA.

  • Animated number counters (15+, 5k+, 98%)
  • Hero badge: gentle float/bounce animation
  • Section headings slide in before cards stagger
  • Entrance animations on ALL sections currently missing them
C

Polished & Cinematic

Rich entrance choreography: hero text reveals word-by-word, a slow ken-burns on the hero image, parallax depth on background blobs, and a smooth sliding testimonial carousel. The kind of feel you'd see on a premium private clinic site.

  • Word-by-word or line-by-line hero text reveal
  • Ken-burns effect on hero image (slow zoom)
  • Auto-sliding testimonial carousel with transitions
  • Parallax scrolling on decorative blobs

Current state: The site already has hero entrance animations, mobile menu transitions, stats card stagger, and domain card stagger. It's missing entrance animations for the Services section, Testimonials, CTA, and Contact — and the hero badge CSS class animate-bounce-subtle is defined but never declared in index.css.
