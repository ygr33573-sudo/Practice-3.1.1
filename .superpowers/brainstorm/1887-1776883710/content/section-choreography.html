How should sections reveal as you scroll?

When a section like "Services" or "Testimonials" comes into view — does the heading animate in before the cards, or does everything fade in together?

A

Heading first, then cards

The section heading and subtitle slide in slightly ahead of the cards. Creates a clear visual hierarchy — you read the label, then the content cascades in below it.

↑ Heading fades in (t=0ms)
↑ Subtitle fades in (t=80ms)
↑ Card 1 (t=160ms)
↑ Card 2 (t=240ms)
↑ Card 3 (t=320ms)
B

Everything together, cards stagger

The heading is already visible (no animation), and the cards stagger in as the section scrolls into view. Cleaner, less orchestrated — the content is the focus, not the reveal.

↑ Heading — static, always visible
↑ Card 1 fades in (t=0ms)
↑ Card 2 (t=80ms)
↑ Card 3 (t=160ms)

This applies to: Services, Testimonials, CTA, and Contact sections — the four sections currently lacking scroll animations.
